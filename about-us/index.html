History

Development of our first version of PHPfox started in the early months of 2005 by our founder Raymond Benc.

At the time Raymond ran a web development company that focused on starting up social networks for his clients. It was at a time when Myspace and Friendster ruled the market.

With the popularity of the product, just a few months into his venture it was best to transform the company into a premium product distributor.
